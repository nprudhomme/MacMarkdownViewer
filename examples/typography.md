# Smart Typography & Emojis

## Smart Quotes

"Double quotes become curly" and 'single quotes too' when smartypants is enabled.

A nested example: "She said, 'Hello, world!'" and it looks great.

## Dashes

An em dash---like this one---is used for interruptions or asides.

An en dash is used for ranges: pages 10--15, years 2020--2025.

## Ellipses

Trailing off... is common in writing. Three dots become a single ellipsis character...

"I wonder..." she thought, looking at the horizon...
